## How to Play
1. Choose your move:
   - 1 for Rock
   - 2 for Paper
   - 3 for Scissor
2. Computer makes its choice
3. Winner of round is determined
4. First to win 5 rounds wins the game!

## Game Rules
- Rock beats Scissor
- Scissor beats Paper
- Paper beats Rock
